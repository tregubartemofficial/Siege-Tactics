# Audio Assets

This folder contains sound effects and background music for Siege Tactics.

## Required Assets

### Sound Effects (`/assets/sounds/`)
- `trebuchet-fire.mp3` - Weapon fire sound (used for all weapon types)
  - Recommended: Short trebuchet launching sound (~1-2 seconds)
  - Format: MP3, 44.1kHz
  - Volume should be normalized

### Background Music (`/assets/music/`)
- `River walk.mp3` - Main gameplay background music
  - Should loop seamlessly
  - Recommended: Atmospheric, calm ambient track
  - Format: MP3, 44.1kHz
  - Duration: 2-5 minutes (will loop)

## Where to Find Free Assets

### Sound Effects:
- Freesound.org - https://freesound.org (search for "trebuchet" or "catapult")
- Zapsplat.com - https://www.zapsplat.com
- Mixkit.co - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/

### Music:
- Kevin MacLeod (incompetech.com) - Royalty-free music
- Purple Planet Music - https://www.purple-planet.com
- Bensound.com - https://www.bensound.com
